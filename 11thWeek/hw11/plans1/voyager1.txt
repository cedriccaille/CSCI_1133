Voyager 1 is a space probe that was launched by NASA on September 5, 1977. Part of the Voyager program to study the outer Solar System, Voyager 1 was launched 16 days after its twin, Voyager 2.
Having operated for 43 years, 2 months and 3 days as of November 8, 2020 UTC, the spacecraft still communicates with the Deep Space Network to receive routine commands and to transmit data to Earth.
